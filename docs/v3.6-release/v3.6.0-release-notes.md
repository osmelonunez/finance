# Finance v3.6.0 - Banking and Loan Integration

This release consolidates banks, accounts, and cards in the **Banking** workspace and integrates loan information without mixing debt, borrowed-capital usage, and available balances.

## Main changes

- Cards now belong to an account through migration `015_cards_linked_to_accounts.sql`.
- The Relationships view represents the Bank → Account → Card hierarchy with direct connectors.
- Bank details show associated loans and KPIs for principal, outstanding debt, amortized amount, and monthly payments.
- Banks that only have loans show a no-balance-information state without creating fictitious accounts.
- Bank charts count loan payments, including principal and interest, but do not count borrowed-capital usages as personal spending.
- Localized monetary formatting, Spanish and English translations, and existing navigation are preserved.
- ING demo data includes three accounts and four correctly linked cards.

## Compatibility and validation

- Existing cards without an assigned account are preserved for a compatible transition.
- Automated coverage includes routes, permissions, calculations, empty states, relationships, integrity, and pagination.
