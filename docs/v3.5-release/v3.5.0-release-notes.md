# Finance v3.5.0 - Accounts and Cards 2.0

## Summary

This release turns banks, accounts, and cards into an independent workspace with financial indicators, entity relationships, detail views, and movement history. It also strengthens data integrity and makes amounts easier to read throughout the application.

## Accounts and cards

- New top-level `Accounts & Cards` workspace at `/payment-methods`, separate from Management.
- Internal navigation through `KPI`, `Relationships`, `Banks`, `Accounts`, and `Cards`.
- One contextual form for creating a bank, account, or card.
- A bank is required when creating or editing an account or card.
- Accounts and cards can only be linked to active banks.
- Accounts and cards are automatically deactivated when their bank is deactivated.
- Accounts and cards with associated movements cannot be deleted.
- Redirects preserve compatibility with the former Management routes.

## KPI and relationships

- Active bank, account, and card indicators together with monthly spending.
- Financial summary selectable by bank, account, or card.
- Year selector for consulting previous financial years.
- Monthly, annual, total, and combined spending charts.
- Clear empty states when the selected scope contains no spending.
- `Relationships` view ordered by the number of accounts and cards linked to each bank.

## Details and history

- New detail views for banks, accounts, and cards.
- Monthly, annual, and total spending metrics where applicable.
- Associated bank, status, reference, and movement count.
- Movement history paginated in server-loaded groups of ten.
- Direct access to the expense list filtered by the current entity.
- Accounts & Cards navigation remains available from every detail view.

## Expenses and presentation

- Independent expense filters for bank, account, and card.
- Inactive entities remain available in filters so historical records can still be consulted.
- Movement creation and duplication are limited to active accounts and cards.
- Locale-aware monetary and numeric formatting throughout the application.
- Language-appropriate digit grouping in tables, indicators, axes, labels, and tooltips.

## Compatibility

- No database migration is required.
- Existing data is not modified or removed.
- Former routes remain compatible through redirects.
- The production compose exposes the service on port `5000`.
